🌱 Our Story

Born in Tamil Nadu, Grown for India

Moringai started in 2019 when our founder's grandmother fell ill and her village doctor prescribed fresh murungai keerai. We couldn't find a single pure, trustworthy source online. So we built one.

Today we work directly with 200+ farmers in Dindigul and Namakkal, process everything in our own FSSAI-licensed facility, and ship to every pin code in India.

2019
Founded in Chennai. First 100 orders fulfilled from a home kitchen.
2021
NABL certification achieved. Partnered with 50 Tamil Nadu farmers.
2023
Crossed 25,000 customers. Launched capsules and oil range.
2025
50,000+ customers. Pan-India distribution. Zero plastic packaging.
