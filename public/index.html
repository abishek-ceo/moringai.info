Our Story

Born in Tamil Nadu's Moringa Fields

Moringai was born from a simple conviction: India's most ancient superfood deserved a world-class platform. Our founders, raised in the moringa-rich villages of Tamil Nadu, watched this miraculous tree be overlooked by modern wellness markets.

We set out to change that. By building direct relationships with organic farmers and establishing rigorous quality standards, we've grown Moringai into India's most trusted moringa brand.

2020
Started with 12 farming families and a single moringa powder product.
2021
Received NABL lab certification. Expanded to 5 products and 50+ farmers.
2022
Pan-India shipping launched. Crossed 10,000 customers milestone.
2026
